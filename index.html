Fabrication

We fabricate tools, machines and equipment. We endeavour to make our products cost effective, environment friendly, time efficient and sustainable.

Design

We turn our ideas into products and consider the design process as one of the most important stage in building our machines, considering aesthetics, functionality and their economical aspects.

Installation

We assist and install your new and customized equipment, ensuring that it is properly and professionally installed. We help in technical issues, troubleshooting and guidance for proper equipment operation.

Patents

We give assistance to fellow inventors who wish to apply for patent of their invention or innovation. We finance prototyping of inventions that our working group deem commercially viable.

Consultation

We will ensure that your custom made tools and equipment are properly installed, maintained and in top operating order within the specified warranty period.

Mission 1

Primarily to invent/innovate tools, machines, equipment, systems and processes for food production, agriculture and small scale mining.

Invent Innovate

Our vision is to be the invention and innovation hub of the Cordilleras that would make Cordilleran industries more efficient, productive, eco-friendly and sustainable.

Mission 2

Secondly to produce, improve and promote less costly, more productive, environmentally friendly and sustainable tools, machines and equipment.
